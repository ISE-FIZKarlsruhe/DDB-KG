Overview

The German Digital Library Data Model (DDB-O) represents cultural heritage holdings within the DDB into a knowledge graph to enable efficient search and retrieval of all objects. DDB-O consists of domain-specific ontologies that satisfy the modeling requirements of the DDB's heterogeneous and voluminous collection of metadata for CHOs. For each of the domains, documentation is available here.
